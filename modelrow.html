Ducati MotoGP Team Achievements

Ducati Motor Holding S.p.A. is an Italian motorcycle manufacturer headquartered in the Borgo Panigale district of Bologna. Ducati is owned by the German automobile manufacturer Audi AG. Learn More...

Ducati was founded in 1926 in Bologna. The company's founders were brothers Adriano and Marcello Ducati. Both were interested in radio technology, so the company's first focus was on radio production. Learn More...

The Ducati 916 debuted in the early 1990s. The motorcycle was designed by Italian designer Massimo Tamburini, who also designed the Bimota series of motorcycles, as well as the MV Agusta F4 and Brutale. Learn More...

In 2000, the Testatretta model was launched. During its development, every engine component was computer-machined to reduce weight and increase strength. The resulting even more advanced engine, producing 135 hp (10,200 rpm), was installed in the new Ducati 996R. Learn More...
